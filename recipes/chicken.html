Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Description:

These tasty air fryer chicken thighs have juicy meat and crispy skin; you will love this quick chicken recipe that is ready for the table in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Spices and seasonings

How to Make Chicken Thighs In the Air Fryer Step-By-Step:

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make air fryer chicken thighs:

  1. Pat the thighs dry and brush the skins with olive oil.
  2. Make the spice blend and sprinkle it all over the thighs.
  3. Cook in the preheated air fryer until the skin is brown and the juices run clear.
